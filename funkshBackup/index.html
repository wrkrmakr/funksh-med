INVENTOR

KYLE BOISELLE

Growing up in an active and outdoors loving family, athletics and extreme sports were simply a part of my DNA. Definitely a professional hard working family who liked to have fun. Being exposed to their medical, mechanical and entrepreneurial professions have certainly influenced me and become a part of my future.

Over the last 20 years, I've been a product sales specialist and trainer in the fitness and tech sales sectors, and have represented some of the worlds most cutting edge technology manufacturers. I've been fortunate as a by product, to have worked with and learned many things from world class athletes, doctors and other technical specialists. The effects of exposure to industry secrets have definitely kept me young and fit.

I've been to 17 cities of the world and lived in five, which has had an impact on my view of the world and ability to communicate to and embrace it's people. I lived in India, Calgary, Los Angeles and now Toronto.

With a purpose, I was always very resourceful, whether it was coming up with ideas or strategies that solved problems, or modifying and inventing something. It seems natural that I would end up founding a company like FUNKSH.

When I started this company, I envisioned developing functional & innovative products and tools for everyday use. Practical technology is important in our lives because it allows us to focus on and spend time on the important parts of it. The easier it is to use, the more accessible it is for everyone.

My goal is continue to develop products that benefit mankind, with FUNKSH as the platform and continue developing projects and relationships in other areas
